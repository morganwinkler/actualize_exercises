Witches Brew

A delightful seasonal drink

Ingredients Needed

  • Rat's Tail
  • Rotten Fish
  • Sage
  • Pumpkin Guts
  • Butter Beer

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients
  2. Simmer butter beer in cauldron over medium fire for 10 mins
  3. Add the rat's tail, rotten fish, and pumpkin guts
  4. Increase fire to high heat and bring to a boil
  5. Once boiling, reduce fire to low and simmer for 10 more mins
  6. Add sage, sip & enjoy
